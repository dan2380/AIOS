## 2. What They Do

- **NuScale Power Module (NPM)** — 77 MWe pressurized light-water small modular reactor
- **Reference plants:** VOYGR-4 (308 MWe), VOYGR-6 (462 MWe), VOYGR-12 (924 MWe)
- **NRC certification status:** 50 MWe certified Jan 2023 (first ever in US). **77 MWe Standard Design Approval received May 29, 2025** (ahead of schedule). VOYGR-12 plant-level application now pending.
- **Manufacturing:** Doosan Enerbility (Changwon, South Korea) — primary forging/heavy components. Doosan invested ~$104M cumulatively (2019, 2021).
- **Partners:** Fluor (EPC, but exiting equity stake); ENTRA1 Energy (exclusive global commercialization partner since 2024).
- **Real differentiation:** Only US-NRC-certified SMR design. That moat is genuine, but narrowing.
