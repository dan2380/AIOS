## 7. Bull vs Bear

**Bull (3):**
1. **Only fuel-cell platform actually shipping MW-scale AI-DC power today.** Oracle's 90-day deployment cycle is the killer feature in a grid-constrained market.
2. **Financial inflection has happened.** $20B backlog, GAAP profitable, +$73M OCF, $2.5B cash. Real revenue distinguishes BE from every other "AI power" story.
3. **Capacity-constrained, not demand-constrained.** Fremont 2x expansion = mechanical revenue doubling regardless of further bookings.

**Bear (3):**
1. **Valuation already prices execution.** ~$80B MC on $3.6B FY26 rev = ~22x sales. Any guide-down on Q2 prints causes a 20-30% gap down.
2. **Natural-gas-fed SOFC is not "clean."** Hyperscalers with net-zero pledges face Scope-1 emissions questions; carbon-capture retrofits are aspirational.
3. **Aschenbrenner's massive AI-hardware put book signals he hedged the entire AI trade.** BE is a relative-strength survivor, but if AI capex rolls, fuel-cell names won't be immune.
