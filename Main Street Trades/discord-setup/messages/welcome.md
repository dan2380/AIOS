# Welcome to Main Street.

Glad you're here. Pull up a chair.

Main Street Trades is a paid Discord for retail traders who want **signal, not screaming**. Every alert comes with a thesis attached. Every closed trade lands on a public scoreboard — wins **and** losses. If a call goes south, you see it. That's the deal.

## How the server is laid out

The sidebar splits into two clear buckets:

🛠️ **Paid Tools / Signals** — the data feeds the machines watch for you. Unusual options volume, congressional + insider trade disclosures, earnings calendar, macro events (CPI, FOMC, NFP), and the pre-market brief.

🤝 **Partner Analysts** — the humans giving picks, each under their own header. First up: **Charlie Plattus** (founder of Zip Trader — options swing setups). More partner analysts coming.

Plus the rest:
- 💬 **Discussion** — day-trading, options, wins, losses, off-topic.
- 🎙 **Live Rooms** — voice, active at market open, FOMC days, after hours.
- 🎁 **Perks** — member broker bonuses and free-trial drops.
- 💎 **VIP Only** — locked unless you're on the top tier.
